About

My Name is Kendrick. I am young composer/arranger. I like to upload my scores to different platforms for people to hear, buy, and hopefully perform at different events. I compose/arrange mostly Marching Band, Wind Ensemble - Beginning Band, and Full Orchestrations that range from The Box by Roddy Ricch to The Batman Theme orchestration and even personal projects that I work on throughout the year.
