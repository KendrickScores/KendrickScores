About

Hi. My name is Kendrick. I am a young composer/arranger. I like to upload my scores to different platforms for people to hear, buy, and hopefully perform at different events. I compose/arrange mostly Marching Band, Wind Ensemble - Beginning Band, and Full Orchestrations that range from The Box by Roddy Ricch to The Batman Theme orchestration and even personal projects that I work on throughout the year. My main goal is to expand my portfolio to hopefully one day be a composer of a major movie or TV Show. Every project that I do starts with me building up the main theme and going off of that. I try to make my music as unique as I possibly can so when you hear my music you know it's from me and me only.

Why

One question I get a lot is why? Why do I do this? I do this because music can be the answer to a lot of problems going on in people's lives. I release arrangements that can be heard without purchasing and always available to listen to at any time/anywhere. Music is what my life revolves around. Not only do I write/arrange music. I also make beats, play two main instruments, and have been around music all my life, so I know what people want to hear. Music is also a great stress reliever so if I can help someone I will try anyway I can!
